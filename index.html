Free Beach Walks!

Welcome to a free community driven website that highlights and suggests fantastic beach walks along the south cost of England.

We are constantly on the lookout for new and fun beach walks and encourage our community to offer suggestions for new beach walks.

If you do have any suggested routes for our team to review please ether use the link at the top of the website or alternatively the link here - Submit Beach Walks to submit your suggested routes. The team will review and provide you with an update as soon as possible.

We do have social media accounts that we would suggest you follow to stay up to date with the latest information, pictures and videos. We encourage our communicate to share and post pictures of all the fun you're having while exploring the beach walks!
