Boscombe & Bournemouth Beach Walk

Route

This is a nice and simple route that is easy to follow. This route starts from the Boscombe pier. You will then turn towards Bournemouth and simply walk straight along the beach for thirty minutes.

Parking

Free parking is located on “Boscombe Overcliff Drive” & "Southbourne Overcliff Drive" roads via marked parking bays.
